# Privacy Policy — Markdowned

**Last updated: July 2, 2026**

Markdowned does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data. Everything
the extension does happens locally in your browser.

## No network requests

Markdowned makes **zero requests to any server**. It does not request host
permissions, does not load remote code, and contains no analytics, tracking,
or advertising components.

## Page access

The extension reads the content of a web page **only** when you click its
toolbar icon and press *Convert page*. The page's HTML is processed entirely
on your device to produce the Markdown output shown in the popup. The content
is never transmitted anywhere.

## Local storage

`chrome.storage.local` is used to store, on your device only:

- your output preferences (heading style, bullet marker, link style, image
  handling, scope, code-block style, frontmatter toggle), and
- your most recent conversion result, so it can be restored when the popup is
  reopened on the same page.

This data never leaves your browser. It is removed when you uninstall the
extension, and you can also clear it at any time via Chrome's
*Clear browsing data → Hosted app data* or by removing and reinstalling the
extension.

## Third parties

No user data is sold or transferred to third parties, used for purposes
unrelated to the extension's single purpose (converting web pages to
Markdown), or used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the update will be published at this URL with a
new "Last updated" date. Given the extension's design (no data collection at
all), material changes are not anticipated.
